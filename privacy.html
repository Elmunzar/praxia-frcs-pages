Praxia FRCS — Privacy Policy

Tr&Orth exam revision

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what Praxia FRCS (“the app”, “we”) does — and does not — do with your information.

The short version

We do not collect your personal data. The app works entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising and no third-party trackers, and we operate no servers that receive your data.

What stays on your device

Your name (if you enter one) and your practice history — which questions you answered and your scores — are stored locally on your device so the app can show your progress. This information never leaves your device through us.

iCloud sync (optional, when available)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your progress is stored in your own private iCloud account so it is available across your devices. This uses Apple’s iCloud under Apple’s terms; we cannot see or access the contents of your iCloud.

Purchases

The app is free to start. If optional paid features are introduced, any purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment-card details; Apple handles billing under its own terms.

Children

Praxia FRCS is intended for medical professionals and trainees and is not directed at children.

Your choices

You can clear your stored progress at any time from within the app. Deleting the app removes its on-device data; data you placed in your own iCloud is managed through your Apple account.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email ebappdevelopment@gmail.com.

Praxia FRCS is an independent revision aid. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any Royal College, the Intercollegiate Specialty Board, or any examination body, and it does not provide medical advice.
