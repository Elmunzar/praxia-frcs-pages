Frequently asked

What is Praxia FRCS?
A revision app for the UK FRCS (Tr&Orth) exam — Single Best Answer and Extended Matching questions, with timed practice, difficulty grades, progress analytics and study planning.
Is it affiliated with the Royal Colleges or the exam board?
No. Praxia FRCS is an independent revision aid. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any Royal College, the Intercollegiate Specialty Board, or any examination body, and it does not provide medical advice.
How is my data handled?
Your name and your practice progress stay on your device. We don’t run accounts, analytics or trackers. See our Privacy Policy.
How do I report a question I think is wrong?
Email us the question and what you’d expect — corrections from practising surgeons are very welcome.
How much does it cost?
Praxia FRCS is free to start. Additional features may later be offered via an optional subscription.
